Target Audience

All residents of the community, including those who are unemployed and seeking employment or who want to start their own business, business owners seeking to expand their enterprises, and those seeking education for better employment in Yaounde or online, are the target audience. This website will also be aimed at anyone looking to launch or market their business. It also goes after people who want to join the chamber. Additionally, it aims to educate those who are interested in entrepreneurship.
